Title

Our aim in this project to construct a balloon powered car which we will test.

Introduction

We want to show you that car Which we will create will pushed forward by air. We will see how fast and far it will go.

Procedure

1)We will do some research on balloon-powered car, we will do an internet image or video research.

2)Then we will make a sketch of design of our car and materials that we used.

3) Many balloon-powered car designs attach the neck of the balloon to a "bendy" straw using a rubber band. This makes it easier to attach the balloon to our car, to inflate the balloon, and to aim the direction of the air escaping the balloon.

4) Then we will start building our car by using all the information that we mentioned before

5) Finally, when we will finish our car, we are going to test it.
